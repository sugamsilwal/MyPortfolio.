About Me

My name is Sugam Silwal, a 24-year-old DevOps engineer currently residing in Kathmandu. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Kathmandu Engineering College, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in technology and problem-solving skills.

I am passionate about all things DevOps. I enjoy working with various tools and technologies to streamline the software development and deployment processes. My role as a DevOps engineer at Amnil Technologies allows me to collaborate with cross-functional teams, automate workflows, and optimize the infrastructure to deliver efficient and reliable software solutions.

Outside of work, I have diverse interests. I am an avid music lover and find solace in different genres of music. It serves as a source of inspiration and relaxation for me. Additionally, I am a sports enthusiast, and I actively engage in various sports activities during my free time. Sports not only keep me physically fit but also teach me valuable lessons in teamwork, discipline, and perseverance.

Originally from Chitwan, I made the transition to Kathmandu to pursue my education and career opportunities. The bustling city offers a vibrant tech community and ample opportunities for personal and professional growth.

I am always eager to expand my knowledge and stay updated with the latest trends in the DevOps field. I enjoy learning new technologies, attending tech meetups, and participating in online forums to exchange ideas and insights with like-minded professionals.

In summary, I am a driven and enthusiastic DevOps engineer with a strong educational background in computer engineering. With a passion for DevOps, love for music, and an active lifestyle, I strive to contribute to the technological advancements and make a positive impact in the industry.
